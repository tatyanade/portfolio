Art && Code : Homemade

In January 2021 I participated as a speaker at the Art && Code: Homemade conference, hosted by the STUDIO for Creative Inquiry, an online festival celebrating and showcasing "casual talks by creators we admire, who work with digital tools and crafty approaches to make things that preserve the magic of something homemade."

I gave a talk about my practice, focusing on my Generative Overcoats, Handmade Animatronics, and work with Algorats. View talk below!
